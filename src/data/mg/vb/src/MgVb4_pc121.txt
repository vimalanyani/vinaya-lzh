<h2>Origin Story</h2>


<lzh>佛住毘舍離。爾時跋陀羅比丘尼到親里家共兄弟姊妹兒於屏處坐。比丘尼嫌言。云何出家人。與俗人隱處坐。猶如俗人。
以是因緣語大愛道。乃至佛言。從今已後。不聽共男子屏處坐乃至已聞者當重聞</lzh>

The Buddha was staying in Vesāli. At that time, Bhaddā bhikkhunī went to her relatives’ house, and sat in a secluded place together with her brothers’ and sisters’ sons. The bhikkhunīs were upset and said: “Why does a renunciate sit with lay people in a hidden place, like a lay person?”
For this reason, they spoke with Mahāpajāpatī, … The Buddha said: “From now on, it’s no longer allowed to sit together with a man in a secluded place.” … Those who have already heard it should listen again:




<h2>Final Ruling</h2>


<lzh>若比丘尼與丈夫屏處坐波夜提。</lzh>

If a bhikkhunī sits with a man in a secluded place, it’s a pācittiya.”




<h2>Explanation</h2>


<lzh>波夜提者如上比丘中廣說。是故世尊說。</lzh>

This pācittiya is explained in detail above in the rule about “a bhikkhu”. This is what the World-honored One said.
