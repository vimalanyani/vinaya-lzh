<lzh>食於比丘不淨比丘尼淨。比丘尼不淨比丘淨。比丘得使比丘尼授食。除金銀及錢五生種火淨。比丘尼得從比丘受食。除金銀及錢火淨五生種。</lzh>

Food that is impure for bhikkhus may be pure for bhikkhunīs. Food that is impure for bhikkhunīs may be pure for bhikkhus. A bhikkhu may have a bhikkhunī offer food, but not gold, silver, money, and the five kinds of purifications of living seeds by fire (etc.). <note>Fruits have to be made allowable for consumption by monastics through a procedure that symbolically damages the seeds. This can be done with five methods, one of them being with fire.</note> A bhikkhunī may accept food from a bhikkhu, but not gold, silver, money, and the five kinds of purifications of living seeds by fire (etc.).
