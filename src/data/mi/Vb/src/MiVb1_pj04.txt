<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時優波離復問佛：「世尊已為諸比丘結戒：『若比丘，不知、不見過人法，乃至不共住。』我當云何持？」佛言：「應作二部僧持。從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, Upāli asked the Buddha again: “The World-honored One has laid down the precept for the bhikkhus: ‘If a bhikkhu doesn’t know and doesn’t see a superhuman state … doesn’t live in community.’ How should I remember this?” The Buddha said: “You should remember it as to be practised by both sanghas. <h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，不知、不見過人法，聖利滿足，自稱我如是知，如是見。是比丘尼後時，若問、若不問，為出罪，求清淨故，作是言：『我不知言知、不見言見，虛誑妄語。』除增上慢，是比丘尼得波羅夷，不共住。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī doesn’t know and doesn’t see a superhuman state, the completion of a noble attainment, and praises herself: “I know this, I see this,” and if that bhikkhunī at a later time, whether questioned or not questioned, in order to clear the offense and to seek purification, says this: “I not knowing said I know, not seeing said I see. It was empty, untrue, false speech,” except in a case of superiority conceit, that bhikkhunī incurs a pārājika, and doesn’t live in community.’”
