<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼，與比丘尼、式叉摩那、沙彌尼，更相覆眠，相見形體，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs slept under the same cover as other bhikkhunīs, sikkhamānās, or sāmaṇerīs. They saw each other’s bodies, … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above. From now on, this precept should be recited thus: 


<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，與比丘尼、式叉摩那、沙彌尼，更相覆眠，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī sleeps under the same cover as another bhikkhunī, a sikkhamānā, or a sāmaṇerī, it’s a pācittiya.’”
