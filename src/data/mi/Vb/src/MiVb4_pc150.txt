<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼，與比丘尼、式叉摩那、沙彌尼同衣臥，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs lay down with a bhikkhunī, a sikkhamānā, or a sāmaṇerī on the same robe … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above. From now on, this precept should be recited thus: 


<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，與比丘尼、式叉摩那、沙彌尼同衣臥，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī lies down with a bhikkhunī, a sikkhamānā, or a sāmaṇerī on the same robe, it’s a pācittiya.’”
