<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼遮受迦絺那衣，諸比丘尼待久不至，妨廢行道。諸長老比丘尼種種呵責，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs obstructed the acceptance of the kaṭhina robe. The (other) bhikkhunīs waited for a long time but they didn’t come, and they were prevented from practising the path. The senior bhikkhunīs criticized them in all kinds of ways, … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above. From now on, this precept should be recited thus: 


<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，遮受迦絺那衣，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī obstructs the acceptance of the kaṭhina robe, it’s a pācittiya.’”


<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>若病，若不聞，不犯。</lzh>
If she’s sick, or if she hasn’t heard about it, the offense isn’t committed.
