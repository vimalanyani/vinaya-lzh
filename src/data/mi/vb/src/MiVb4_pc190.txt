<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，誦外道呪術，若教人誦，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī recites non-Buddhist magic, or teaches others to recite it, it’s a pācittiya.’”
