<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼以欲心，自觀形體，生愛欲意，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs looked at their own bodies with lustful minds. Intentions of sexual desire arose. … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above. From now on, this precept should be recited thus: 


<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，以欲心，自觀形體，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī looks at her own body with a lustful mind, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<lzh>式叉摩那、沙彌尼，突吉羅。</lzh>
For a sikkhamānā and a sāmaṇerī, it’s a dukkaṭa.
