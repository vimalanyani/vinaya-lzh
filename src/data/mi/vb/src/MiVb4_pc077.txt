<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼與比丘獨露處，共立、共語，生染著心，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs stood and talked with bhikkhus alone outdoors, and defiled attachment arose in their minds. … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above. From now on, this precept should be recited thus: 


<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，與比丘獨露處，共立、共語，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī stands and talks with a bhikkhu alone outdoors, it’s a pācittiya.’”
