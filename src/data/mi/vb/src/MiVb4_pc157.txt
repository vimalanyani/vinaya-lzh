<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼著卑身衣，使形𦟛纖得中，生愛欲心，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs wore corsets to make their fat or thin bodies become average. Sexual desire arose in their minds. … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above. From now on, this precept should be recited thus: 


<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，著卑身衣，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī wears a corset, it’s a pācittiya.’”


<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>式叉摩那、沙彌尼，突吉羅。</lzh>
For a sikkhamānā and a sāmaṇerī, it’s a dukkaṭa.

<lzh>不犯如上。</lzh>
The non-offenses are as above.
