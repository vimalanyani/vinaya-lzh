<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，與白衣及外道獨露處，共立、共語，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī stands and talks with a lay person or a non-Buddhist renunciate alone outdoors, it’s a pācittiya.’”
