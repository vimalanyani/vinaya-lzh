<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼，雖滿二歲學戒尼，而聾盲瘖瘂種種諸病，與受具足戒。諸長老比丘尼見，種種訶責，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說，滿十二歲已嫁女中說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs gave the full ordination to nuns who, although they had trained in the precepts for two years, were deaf, blind, mute, and had all kinds of diseases. When the senior bhikkhunīs saw it, they criticized them in all kinds of ways, … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above in ‘a 12 year old married woman’. <h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，滿二歲學戒尼，僧不作羯磨，與受具足戒，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī gives the full ordination to a nun who has trained in the precepts for two years, when the sangha hasn’t carried out a formal act, it’s a pācittiya.’”
