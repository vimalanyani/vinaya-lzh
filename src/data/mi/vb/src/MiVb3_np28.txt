<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，自為是，事從眾多居士乞；自作餘用者，尼薩耆波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī asks for something for herself from many householders, and then uses it herself for another purpose, it’s a nissaggiya pācittiya.’”
