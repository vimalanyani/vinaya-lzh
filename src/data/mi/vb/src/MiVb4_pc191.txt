<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時諸比丘尼眾自授具足戒，彼愚闇無知不能學戒。諸長老比丘尼聞，種種呵責，乃至今為諸比丘尼結戒，亦如上說。「從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the bhikkhunīs gave the full ordination to a group. They were ignorant and without knowledge, and unable to train in the precepts. When the senior bhikkhunīs heard it, they criticized them in all kinds of ways, … “I now lay down a precept for the bhikkhunīs, as explained above. From now on, this precept should be recited thus: 


<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，一眾授具足戒，波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī gives the full ordination to a group, it’s a pācittiya.’”


<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>發心，乃至白四羯磨未竟，突吉羅。竟，和尚，波逸提；餘師僧，突吉羅。</lzh>
If she thinks about it, up to when the formal act with a motion as fourth isn’t finished yet, it’s a dukkaṭa. When it’s finished, it’s a pācittiya for the preceptor. For the other teachers and the sangha, it’s a dukkaṭa.
