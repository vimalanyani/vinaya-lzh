<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，為僧、為是事，從眾多居士乞；而餘用者，尼薩耆波逸提。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī asks for something for the sangha from many householders, and then uses it for another purpose, it’s a nissaggiya pācittiya.’”
