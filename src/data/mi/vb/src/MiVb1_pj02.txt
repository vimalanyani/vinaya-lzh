<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>爾時長老優波離復問佛：「世尊已為諸比丘結戒：『若比丘，若聚落、若空地，盜心不與取，乃至不共住。』我當云何持？」佛言：「應作二部僧持。從今是戒應如是說：</lzh>
At that time, the venerable Upāli asked the Buddha again: “The World-honored One has laid down the precept for the bhikkhus: ‘If a bhikkhu in a village or an empty place takes what isn’t given with the intention to steal it, … doesn’t live in community.’ How should I remember this?” The Buddha said: “You should remember it as to be practised by both sanghas. <h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若比丘尼，若聚落、若空地，盜心不與取。若王、若大臣，若捉、若縛、若殺、若擯，語言：『汝賊！汝癡！』是比丘尼得波羅夷，不共住。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī in a village or an empty place takes what isn’t given with the intention to steal it, and if the king or a high official either arrest, or bind, or kill, or banish her, and say: “You criminal, you fool!,” that bhikkhunī incurs a pārājika, and doesn’t live in community.’”
