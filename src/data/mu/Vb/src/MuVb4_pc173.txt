<lzh>唱歌學處第一百七十三</lzh>
The 173rd training precept: Singing songs.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼詣婆羅門長者家，諸婦人言：「聖者！教我唱歌。」尼便教唱。俗旅見譏，如前所說。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問吐羅難陀：「汝實如此教他唱歌？」答言：「實爾。」世尊訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī came a brahmin householder’s home, and the women said: “Noble one, teach us to sing songs.” The bhikkhunī then taught them to sing. The lay people saw it and were upset, as explained before. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked Thullanandā: “Did you really teach others to sing songs in this way?” She answered: “I really did.” The World-honored One criticized her, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，唱歌者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī sings songs, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀等。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā etc.

<lzh>唱歌者，謂唱歌詞音韻。</lzh>
“Singing songs” means singing songs with words and rhythms.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
