<lzh>每年與出家受近圓學處第一百二十四</lzh>
The 124th training precept: Giving the going forth and the full ordination every year.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼，於每年中與他出家并受近圓，不施名字。若有事至但喚言：「咄！求寂女。咄！正學女。咄！少年。」者。諸苾芻尼聞，告吐羅尼曰：「聖者！何故每年與他出家？」報諸尼曰：「我與繫怨家項與彼出家。」有尼問曰：「誰是怨家？」報曰：「汝即大怨家，於我生不忍。」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī gave the going forth and the full ordination to someone every year, and she didn’t give them a name. If something was going on, she just called them: “Hey, sāmaṇerī! Hey, sikkhamānā! Hey, young one!” The bhikkhunīs heard it and said to Thullā bhikkhunī: “Noble one, why do you give the going forth to someone every year?” She replied to the bhikkhunīs: “I gave them the going forth to tie up their enemies.” A bhikkhunī asked her: “Who are these enemies?” (Thullanandā) replied: “You’re a great enemy. You don’t tolerate me.” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，於每年中，與他出家及受近圓者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī gives the going forth and the full ordination to someone every year, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>於每年中者，謂年年中與他出家及受近圓。</lzh>
“Every year” means giving the going forth and the full ordination to someone each year.

<lzh>釋罪相等，餘說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained before.
