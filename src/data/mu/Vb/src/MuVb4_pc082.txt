<lzh>獨與苾芻屏處立學處第八十二</lzh>
The 82nd training precept: Standing alone with a bhikkhu in a secluded place.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時笈多苾芻尼，與鄔陀夷在屏處立。婆羅門長者及諸男子見已生嫌，共相議曰：「此尼非是寂靜出家，乃與苾芻在屏處立，必作期會。」其不信者作種種譏謗。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責廣如上說，乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Guttā bhikkhunī stood in a secluded place with Udāyī. When the brahmin householders and men had seen them, they got upset and said to each other: “This bhikkhunī hasn’t gone forth for extinguishment. She even stands in a secluded place with a bhikkhu. They must be on a date.” Those without faith slandered her in all kinds of ways. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail above, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，獨與苾芻在屏處立者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī stands alone with a bhikkhu in a secluded place, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂笈多苾芻尼，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Guttā bhikkhunī, or any other bhikkhunī. 

<lzh>苾芻者，謂鄔陀夷苾芻。</lzh>
“A bhikkhu” means Udāyī bhikkhu. 

<lzh>在屏處立者，屏有五種義如上說。</lzh>
“Standing in a secluded place”: There are five kinds of seclusions as explained above. 

<lzh>乃至立者，皆得墮罪。</lzh>
… If they stand, they all incur a pācittiya offense.
