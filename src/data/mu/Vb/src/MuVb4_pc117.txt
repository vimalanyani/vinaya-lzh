<lzh>度惡性女人學處第一百一十七</lzh>
The 117th training precept: Ordaining a woman of bad character.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀苾芻尼，入室羅伐城乞食，見一女人立性多瞋兇麁樂鬪，與餘女人共為諍競，頭髮皆竪作野干鳴，餘人聞聲即便倒地。吐羅尼見作如是念：「我能引彼為出家者，必能與力助我相鬪。」即以方便度彼出家。後於異時，吐羅尼共餘一尼有少諍競，新出家尼默然看住。吐羅尼告曰：「汝不能活，我與出家，何故今時默然而住，不見相助？」尼言：「聖者！我今不知本事，云何相助？」吐羅尼曰：「我若與大世主尼相競，汝可罵言：『私剃頭者』；蓮花色尼，『於六大城衒色自活』；法與尼，『因使得受近圓』；瘦喬答彌，『被他抑令食其子肉者』，當以此詞相助訶罵。」惡性尼聞吐羅尼共他鬪時，調弄諸尼共相鬪諍，眾多尼曰：「誰度如是惡性樂鬪令其出家？」於中答言：「除吐羅尼，誰當度此？」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī entered Sāvatthī on almsround, saw a woman of a hateful character, evil, rough, enjoying to quarrel, fighting with other women. With her hair sticking up, she immitated the howl of a jackal. When other people heard the sound, they fell to the ground. When Thullā bhikkhunī saw her, she thought: “If I can get her to go forth, she’ll certainly support and help me quarrel with others.” Then she used skillful means to give her the going forth. At a later time, Thullā bhikkhunī had a small fight with other bhikkhunīs. The newly-gone forth nun stood there watching silently. Thullā bhikkhunī told her: “Can’t you save me? I’ve given you the going forth. Why do now stand there silently? Didn’t you see that I need help?” The nun said: “Noble one, at the moment, I don’t know the background, so how can I help you?” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “If I fight with Mahāpajāpatī bhikkhunī, you can abuse her: ‘She shaved her head herself.’ To Uppalavaṇṇā bhikkhunī (you can say:) ‘She made a living by showing off her beauty in the six great cities.’ To Dhammadinnā bhikkhunī: ‘She received the full ordination by messenger.’ To Kisāgotamī: ‘She was forced to eat her child’s flesh.’ You should help me and criticize them with these phrases.” When the nun of bad character heard Thullā bhikkhunī quarrel with others, she made fun of the bhikkhunīs who quarreled. Many bhikkhunīs said: “Who ordained such a person of bad character who enjoys quarreling, and let her go forth?” Someone among them answered: “Who except for Thullā bhikkhunī would ordain her?” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，知惡性女人好為鬪諍，與出家并受近圓者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī knows that a woman has a bad character and enjoys quarreling, and gives her the going forth and the full ordination, it’s a pācittiya.’”<note>The pātimokkha just says “the going forth” and doesn’t mention the full ordination.</note>

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>惡性女人者，謂好鬪諍。</lzh>
“A woman of bad character” means that she enjoys quarreling.

<lzh>出家者，謂受求寂并餘學處。</lzh>
“The going forth” means giving the sāmaṇera and other precepts.

<lzh>受近圓者，義如上說。</lzh>
“Giving the full ordination” is as explained above.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣亦同前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
