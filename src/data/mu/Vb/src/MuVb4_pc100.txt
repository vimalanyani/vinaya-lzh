<lzh>二尼同一床臥學處第一百</lzh>
The 100th training precept: Two bhikkhunīs lying down together on one bed.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時惡愛、上愛二苾芻尼同在一床，如男與女共為戲樂。一尼於後遂即有娠，日月既滿生一肉團，諸根手足並皆未有。諸尼聞已擯令出寺。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛言：「且未須擯，當審觀察。將此肉團置於日中，若其消化即非有娠，如不消滅當實有胎。」尼依佛教即置日中，悉皆消散。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then, out of unwholesome desire, hightened desire, two bhikkhunīs were together in one bed, and amused themselves together like a man and a woman. One bhikkhunī later became pregnant, and when the days and months had elapsed, she gave birth to a mass of flesh without sense faculties, hands and feet. When the bhikkhunīs heard it, they expelled her and made her leave the monastery. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha said: “You don’t need to expel her for now. You should closely investigate. You should place this mass of flesh out in the sun. If it dissolves, she wasn’t really pregnant. If it doesn’t dissolve, she was really pregnant.” According to the Buddha’s instructions, the bhikkhunīs placed it out in the sun, and it completely disappeared. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，二尼同一床臥者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If two bhikkhunīs lie down together on one bed, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>如是世尊為諸苾芻尼制學處已，時有眾多苾芻尼，因行日暮從一長者夜求宿處，長者容許與一大床。一尼獨居餘尼更索，長者報言：「家內人多復無餘長，聖者處迮何不同床？」尼曰：「世尊不聽尼同床臥。」由此事故尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛言：「若得大床難舁舉者，尼得同處，當以衣隔繫念而眠，不得相觸，小床安隔亦得同眠。」</lzh>
When the World-honored One had laid down a training precept for the bhikkhunīs like this, a group of bhikkhunīs were wandering, and at dusk, asked a householder for a place to stay overnight. The householder allowed them (to stay) and gave them one large bed. One bhikkhunī stayed there alone, and the other bhikkhunīs asked again. The householder replied: “In this house, there are many people, and there’s no other extra (bed). Why are the noble ones pressing me for unshared beds?” The bhikkhunīs said: “The World-honored One doesn’t allow bhikkhunīs to lie down on a bed together.” Because of this matter, the bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha said: “If they obtain a large bed that’s hard to carry and lift up, bhikkhunīs may stay together. They should make a barrier out of a robe, and sleep with a firm mind. They may not touch each other. If they set up a barrier in a small bed, they may also sleep together.
