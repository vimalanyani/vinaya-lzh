<lzh>作樂學處第一百七十四</lzh>
The 174th training precept: Making music.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼詣豪富家，與其女人歡娛相愛，諸婦人言：「聖者！教我音樂。」尼便教作，俗旅見譏。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問吐羅難陀：「汝實如此教他作樂？」答言：「實爾。」世尊訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī came to a wealthy house, and amused herself with the women. They were fond of each other, and the women said: “Noble one, teach us music.” The bhikkhunī then taught them how to do it. The lay people saw it and were upset. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked Thullanandā: “Did you really teach them to make music in this way?” She answered: “I really did.” The World-honored One criticized her, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，作樂者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī makes music, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀等。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā etc.

<lzh>作樂者，謂作音聲絃管。</lzh>
“Making music” means making sounds with musical instruments.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
