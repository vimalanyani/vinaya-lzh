<lzh>知曾嫁女人年未滿十二與出家學處第一百八</lzh>
The 108th training precept: Knowingly giving the going forth to a married woman who isn’t 12 years old yet.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時愚癡人惡生誅伐釋種，多有釋女無所依怙得為出家，憂愁親戚思念悲泣，後既悟法憂念漸除求受近圓。諸苾芻尼曰：「汝等待年滿二十方受近圓。」白言：「聖者！待滿二十時極久長。」諸尼曰：「若滿二十，即能奉事鄔波馱耶及阿遮利耶。」尼曰：「我等在家事夫營業尚能成辦，今豈不能奉親教師及軌範師耶？」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛言：「若曾嫁女，年滿十二或十八歲者，應與二年正學法方授近圓。應如是與，</lzh>
Location as before. Then foolish people unskillfully killed the Sakyans, and many Sakyan women who had nobody to depend on obtained the going forth. They worried about their relatives, longing for them and weeping with grief. Later they understood the dhamma, gradually gave up grieving, and asked for the full ordination. The bhikkhunīs said: “Wait until you’re 20 years old to receive the full ordination.” (The Sakyan novices) said: “Noble ones, to wait until 20 is such a long time.” The bhikkhunīs said: “When you’re 20, you can serve your preceptors and teachers.” The (novice) nuns said: “As householders, we were fully capable of running our husbands’ households. How could we now not be able to serve our preceptors and teachers?” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha said: “If a married woman is between 12 and 18 years old, you should give her the sikkhamānā rules for two years, and then give her the full ordination. You should give them as follows: 

<h2>Saṅghakamma</h2>

<lzh>僧伽悉集，令彼隨次禮已，於上座前作如是語：『大德尼僧伽聽！我某甲今因事故以尊者某甲為親教師。今從尼僧伽乞六法、六隨法為正學女，願尼僧伽與某甲六法、六隨法正學處，某甲為親教師。是能愍者，願哀愍故。』如是三說。次一苾芻尼作白羯磨：『大德尼僧伽聽！此求寂女某甲年滿十八，某甲為鄔波馱耶，今從苾芻尼僧伽於二年內乞學六法、六隨法。若苾芻尼僧伽時至聽者，苾芻尼僧伽應許苾芻尼僧伽今與求寂女某甲年滿十八，於二年內學六法、六隨法，某甲為鄔波馱耶。白如是。』次作羯磨。</lzh>
The whole sangha should gather and make her pay respect in order. In front of the most senior bhikkhunī, she should say: ‘Venerable bhikkhunīs, may the sangha listen. I, So-and-so, now, because of this matter, take venerable So-and-so as my preceptor. I ask the bhikkhunī sangha for the six rules and the six supplementary rules of sikkhamānās. May the bhikkhunī sangha give So-and-so the six rules and the six supplementary rules of sikkhamānās, with So-and-so as the preceptor. May those capable of kindness have compassion.’ She speaks like this three times. Then one bhikkhunī carries out the motion and the announcement: ‘Venerable bhikkhunīs, may the sangha listen. This 18 year old sāmaṇerī So-and-so, with So-and-so as the preceptor, now asks the bhikkhunī sangha for the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules. If the right time has come for the bhikkhunī sangha to allow this, the bhikkhunī sangha should approve that the bhikkhunī sangha now gives the 18 year old sāmaṇerī So-and-so the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules, with So-and-so as the preceptor. This is the motion.’ Then she carries out the announcement:

<lzh>『大德尼僧伽聽！此求寂女某甲年滿十八，某甲為鄔波馱耶，今從苾芻尼僧伽於二年內乞學六法、六隨法，某甲為鄔波馱耶。苾芻尼僧伽今與求寂女某甲年滿十八，於二年內學六法、六隨法，某甲為鄔波馱耶。若諸具壽聽與求寂女某甲年滿十八，於二年內學六法、六隨法，某甲為鄔波馱耶者默然；若不許者說。』『苾芻尼僧伽已與求寂女某甲年滿十八，於二年內學六法、六隨法，某甲為鄔波馱耶竟。苾芻尼僧伽已聽許，由其默然故，我今如是持。』</lzh>
‘Venerable bhikkhunīs, may the sangha listen. This 18 year old sāmaṇerī So-and-so, with So-and-so as the preceptor, now asks the bhikkhunī sangha for the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules, with So-and-so as the preceptor. The bhikkhunī sangha now gives the 18 year old sāmaṇerī So-and-so the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules, with So-and-so as the preceptor. If the long-lived ones approve to give the 18 year old sāmaṇerī So-and-so the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules, with So-and-so as the preceptor, you should remain silent. If you don’t approve, you should speak.’ ‘The bhikkhunī sangha has given the 18 year old sāmaṇerī So-and-so the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules, with So-and-so as the preceptor. The bhikkhunī sangha has approved, therefore you’re silent. I now remember it like this.’

<lzh>「次應告言：『汝某甲聽！始從今日應學六法：一者不得獨在道行，二者不得獨渡河水，三者不得觸丈夫身，四者不得與男子同宿，五者不得為媒嫁事，六者不得覆尼重罪。』」</lzh>
Then she should say: ‘So-and-so, listen. Starting from today, you should train in the six rules: 1) You may not walk on a path alone. 2) You may not cross a river alone. 3) You may not touch a man’s body. 4) You may not spend the night together with a man. 5) You may not engage in matchmaking activities. 6) You may not conceal a bhikkhunī’s grave offense.’”

<lzh>「復言：『汝某甲聽！始從今日應學六隨法：一者不得捉屬己金銀，二者不得剃隱處毛，三者不得墾掘生地，四者不得故斷生草木，五者不得不受而食。六者不得食曾觸食。』」</lzh>
“She should also say: ‘So-and-so, listen. Starting from today, you should train in the six supplementary rules: 1) You may not touch or possess personal gold and silver. 2) You may not shave the hair in the hidden place. 3) You may not cultivate and dig fertile soil. 4) You may not intentionally damage living grass and trees. 5) You may not eat what hasn’t been offered. 6) You may not eat food that has been touched.’”

<h2>Origin Story (continued)</h2>

<lzh>如是世尊令曾嫁女應滿二年學六法、六隨法正學法已方受近圓。時吐羅難陀尼，未滿十二歲女，與出家并授近圓。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
In this way, the World-honored One allowed married women to complete the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules of the sikkhamānā training, and then receive the full ordination. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī gave the going forth and the full ordination to a woman who wasn’t 12 years old yet. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this:

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，知曾嫁女人年未滿十二，與出家者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī knows that a married woman isn’t 12 years old yet, and gives her the going forth, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼等如上。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” is as above. 

<lzh>曾嫁女者，謂曾適他氏。</lzh>
“A married woman” means she has already gone to another family. 

<lzh>未滿者，謂年未十二。</lzh>
“Not yet” means she’s not 12 years old yet. 

<lzh>與出家者，義如上說。</lzh>
“Giving the going forth” is as explained above. 

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
