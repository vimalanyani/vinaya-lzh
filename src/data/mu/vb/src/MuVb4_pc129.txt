<lzh>不於二部眾三事作隨意學處第一百二十九</lzh>
The 129th training precept: Not doing the invitation ceremony with both sanghas regarding the three things.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時諸苾芻尼夏安居了，於十五日欲作隨意事。餘尼語吐羅難陀曰：「聖者！可來往僧寺中作隨意事。」答曰：「但於此作，何勞往耶？」尼曰：「如世尊說：『苾芻尼應於二部眾中說三事作隨意，謂見聞疑。』」吐羅尼曰：「我是三藏善能問答，豈可不知作隨意事更往二部眾中耶？」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then the bhikkhunīs had finished the rains retreat and wanted to carry out the invitation ceremony on the 15th day. The other bhikkhunīs said to Thullanandā: “Noble one, please come, and go with us to the bhikkhu monastery to carry out the invitation ceremony.” She answered: “Just do it here. Why bother going there?” The bhikkhunīs said: “As the World-honored One has said: ‘A bhikkhunī should carry out the invitation ceremony with both sanghas regarding the three things, namely the seen, the heard, and the suspected.’” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “I can fully answer questions regarding the three baskets. How could I not know how to carry out the invitation ceremony and still go to both sanghas?” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼安居了，不於二部眾中以三事作隨意者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī, when the rains retreat is finished, doesn’t carry out the invitation ceremony with both sanghas regarding the three things, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>安居了者，謂三月安居竟。</lzh>
“When the rains retreat is finished” means when the three months of the rains retreat are over.

<lzh>不於二部眾中者，謂不於僧尼眾內以三事見聞疑作隨意，</lzh>
“Not with both sanghas” means not carrying out the invitation ceremony in the assembly of bhikkhus and bhikkhunīs, regarding the three things, the seen, the heard, and the suspected.

<lzh>餘說如前。</lzh>
The rest is as explained before.
