<lzh>安居滿不遊行學處第一百二</lzh>
The 102nd training precept: Not wandering when the rains retreat is over.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時諸苾芻尼，夏安居竟欲遊人間，告吐羅難陀尼曰：「可遊人間。」吐羅尼曰：「我今何用遊行人間？」諸尼曰：「佛教令去，何因故違？」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then the bhikkhunīs, when the rains retreat was finished, wanted to wander among the people. They said to Thullanandā bhikkhunī: “Let’s wander among the people.” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “What use is it for me to wander among the people now?” The bhikkhunīs said: “The Buddha instructed us to go. Why would you disobey him?” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，夏安居滿，不離舊處人間遊行者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī, when the rains retreat is over, doesn’t leave her old place and wander among the people, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼等如上。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” is as above. 

<lzh>夏安居滿者，謂安居竟。</lzh>
“The rains retreat is over” means the rains retreat is finished. 

<lzh>不離舊處者，謂不往人間遊行。</lzh>
“Not leaving one’s old place” means not going and wandering among the people. 

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
