<lzh>獨向俗家學處第七</lzh>
The 7th training precept: Going to lay houses alone.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼，於晝日中獨一無伴，往俗人舍為長者等說法。諸尼告言：「汝莫晝日獨往他家，恐有淨行難。」吐羅難陀報曰：「汝等不見，賣香男子我以脚蹋口中血出。」諸尼曰：「未必眾人同彼怯弱。」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛以此緣同前集尼，問實訶責，乃至制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī went to lay houses during daytime alone without a companion, and taught the dhamma to householders. The bhikkhunīs said: “Don’t go alone to those houses during daytime. We fear that your celibacy will be in danger.” Thullanandā replied: “Did you not see the male perfume vendor who I kicked with my foot, and drew blood from his mouth?” The bhikkhunīs said: “Other people wouldn’t necessarily be as timid and weak as him.” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha gathered the bhikkhunīs for this reason as before, asked if it was true, and criticized her, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this:

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，獨從尼寺晝向俗家者，僧伽伐尸沙。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī leaves the bhikkhunī monastery and goes to lay houses alone during daytime, it’s a saṅghādisesa.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>苾芻尼者，謂吐羅難陀，或復餘人。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other person.

<lzh>獨行無伴向他俗舍，乃至日沒，犯眾教罪。若與求寂女同去者，犯窣吐羅底也。與正學女同去者，得惡作罪。</lzh>
If she goes to lay houses alone without a companion before sunset, she commits a saṅghādisesa. If she goes together with a sāmaṇerī, she commits a thullaccaya. If she goes together with a sikkhamānā, she incurs a dukkaṭa.
