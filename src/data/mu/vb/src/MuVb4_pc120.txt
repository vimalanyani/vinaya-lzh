<lzh>知學法了不與受近圓學處第一百二十</lzh>
The 120th training precept: Knowing that the training in the rules has been completed, but not giving the full ordination.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼，度他女人出家，與二歲學六法、六隨法。滿已白言：「聖者！可與我受近圓。」便報彼尼：「汝可更學極令通利，當受近圓。」彼尼默住。後於異時，眾多苾芻尼告曰：「汝既學法已滿，何不受近圓耶？」答言：「我已諮請聖者吐羅難陀。報曰：『可更學令通利，當受近圓。』」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī gave the going forth to a woman, and gave her the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules. When it was completed, (the woman) said: “Noble one, please give me the full ordination.” (Thullanandā) replied to that nun: “You should still keep training. It’ll be very profitable for you. You’ll then receive the full ordination.” That nun stood there silently. At a later time, many bhikkhunīs said: “You’ve already completed the training in the rules. Why didn’t you receive the full ordination?” She replied: “I’ve already asked the noble Thullanandā. She replied: ‘You should still keep training. It’ll be profitable for you. You’ll then receive the full ordination.’” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，知女人二歲學六法及六隨法了，不與受近圓者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī knows that a woman has completed the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules, and doesn’t give her the full ordination, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>知女人二歲學者，謂學法已滿不與受近圓。</lzh>
“Knowing that a woman has trained for two years” means having completed the training in the rules, and not giving her the full ordination.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
