<lzh>得多人利迴入己學處第二十九</lzh>
The 29th training precept: Diverting offerings intended for many people to oneself.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。如世尊說：「應作五年六年大會。」時有眾多苾芻尼來，由此因緣，吐羅難陀入室羅伐城，從長者婆羅門妻乞求，得多利養便迴入己。諸尼聞已白諸苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛以此緣，同前集尼問實訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. As the World-honored One had said: “Every five to six years, you should hold a great festival.” Then a great group of bhikkhunīs came. For this reason, Thullanandā entered Sāvatthī and requested things from female brahmin householders. She obtained many offerings and diverted them to herself. When the bhikkhunīs heard this, they told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha gathered the bhikkhunīs for this reason as before, asked if it was true, and criticized her, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>若復苾芻尼，得多人利迴入己者，泥薩祇波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī obtains offerings intended for many people and diverts them to herself, it’s a nissaggiya pācittiya.’”
