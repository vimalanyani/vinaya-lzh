<lzh>年未滿與他出家授近圓學處第一百六</lzh>
The 106th training precept: Giving others the going forth and the full ordination without seniority.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣在室羅伐城。時諸苾芻年未滿十歲，與他出家及受近圓，諸苾芻尼亦然。時十二眾尼門徒極眾，詣六眾住處，六眾告曰：「汝等徒眾極多圍繞。」尼言：「如聖者等與他出家及受近圓，我等亦爾。」問曰：「汝與我等無差殊耶？」答言：「不異。」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
At Sāvatthī. Then bhikkhus who didn’t have 10 vassa yet gave others the going forth and the full ordination. The bhikkhunīs did the same. Then the group-of-twelve bhikkhunīs had very many disciples. They went to the the residence of the group-of-six (bhikkhus), and the group-of-six said: “You’re surrounded by very many disciples.” The bhikkhunīs said: “Just as the noble ones give others the going forth and the full ordination, we’re doing the same.” (The bhikkhus) asked: “Is there no difference between you and us?” (The bhikkhunīs) answered: “There’s no difference.” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，未滿十二歲，與他出家受近圓者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī, who doesn’t have 12 vassa yet, gives others the going forth and the full ordination, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼等如上。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” is as above. 

<lzh>與他出家者，謂受求寂學處。</lzh>
“Giving others the going forth” means the sāmaṇera precepts. 

<lzh>受近圓者，謂白四羯磨。</lzh>
“Giving the full ordination” means with a formal act with a motion as fourth. 

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained before.
