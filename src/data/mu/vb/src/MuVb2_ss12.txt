<lzh>輒作解舉學處第十二</lzh>
The 12th training precept: Just reinstating.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時有苾芻尼名曰亂意，性懷瞋恚，常以惡言共相罵辱，時諸尼眾悉皆嫌賤。彼出乞食，諸尼見無即便共集說其惡行。彼尼有女名曰寂靜，既見諸人說母過惡，乞食來至悉皆向說。彼聞語已更發瞋心，於諸尼眾麁語期刻。眾皆尋問：「誰作如是鬪亂兩邊？」知是寂靜向母陳說，大眾即與寂靜作捨置羯磨。其女啼泣詣於母所，禮足言曰：「諸尼與我作捨置羯磨。」亂意聞已忿怒倍增，作如是語：「願汝總與國人作捨置羯磨。」即將其女出向界外，為作解法。諸尼告言：「眾為汝女作捨置羯磨，汝復何因界外為解？」答曰：「眾強作法，我解何過？」問曰：「汝於何處共誰作法？」答曰：「是我界外共人為解。」尼曰：「豈合如此作解法耶？」答曰：「從合不合我已作訖，何忓汝事？」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛以此事同前集尼問實訶責，乃至制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then there was a bhikkhunī called Confused Mind, who was prone to anger and often abused others with harsh words. Then the whole bhikkhunī sangha disliked her. When she went out on almsround and the bhikkhunīs saw that she wasn’t there, they gathered and spoke about her bad conduct. That bhikkhunī had a daughter called Tranquility. When she saw that people spoke about her mother’s transgressions, she went to her after she had returned from almsround and told her everything. When (the mother) heard what had been said, anger arose in her mind, and she decided to harrass the bhikkhunī sangha with coarse speech. The sangha investigated: “Who caused this conflict between the two sides?” They found out that Tranquility had told her mother, and the great sangha then carried out a formal act of suspension against Tranquility. The daughter cried and went to her mother, paid respect at her feet, and said: “The bhikkhunīs have carried out a formal act of suspension against me.” When Confused Mind heard this, she became even more angry, and said: “May the people of the country carry out a formal act of suspension against you all!<note>I.e. she’s cursing the bhikkhunī sangha.</note>” Then she took her daughter, left the territory, and carried out a procedure of reinstatement. The bhikkhunīs said: “The sangha has carried out a formal act of suspension against your daughter. Why did you reinstate her outside the territory?” She answered: “The sangha did the procedure by force. If I reinstate her, how’s that a transgression?” They asked: “Where and with whom did you carry out the procedure?” She answered: “I reinstated her outside the territory with people.” The bhikkhunīs said: “How could it be suitable to carry out a procedure of reinstatement like this?” She answered: “Suitable or not, I’ve already done it. How is that your business?” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha gathered the bhikkhunīs because of this matter as before, asked if it was true, and criticized her, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this:

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，知苾芻尼被苾芻尼眾為作捨置羯磨，便出界外為作解法者，僧伽伐尸沙。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī knows that the bhikkhunī sangha has carried out a formal act of suspension against a bhikkhunī, and just leaves the territory to carry out a procedure of reinstatement, it’s a saṅghādisesa.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂亂意，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Confused Mind, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>知苾芻尼者，謂是寂靜。</lzh>
“Knowing … against a bhikkhunī” means Tranquility.

<lzh>被尼眾者，謂是如來聲聞尼眾。</lzh>
“The bhikkhunī sangha” means this bhikkhunī sangha of the disciples of the Tathāgata.

<lzh>作捨置羯磨者，謂白四羯磨。</lzh>
“Carrying out a formal act of suspension” means a formal act with a motion as fourth.

<lzh>便出界外為作解法者，謂解羯磨法。得眾教罪，此謂初犯。</lzh>
“Just leaving the territory to carry out a procedure of reinstatement” means the procedure of a formal act of reinstatement. She incurs a saṅghādisesa. These are the immediate offenses.

<lzh>此中犯相其事云何？若苾芻尼輒作如是出界外作解舉者，得眾教罪。</lzh>
Herein, what are the factors for committing this offense? If a bhikkhunī just acts in such a way, leaves the territory, and carries out a reinstatement, she incurs a saṅghādisesa.
