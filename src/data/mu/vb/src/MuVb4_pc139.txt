<lzh>不畜洗裙學處第一百三十九</lzh>
The 139th training precept: Not keeping a bathing skirt.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時有眾多苾芻尼，於阿氏羅河與諸俗女同為洗浴，以指相指，看尼嬭房腰腹𮪲踹等，隨事讚說。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問實訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then there were many bhikkhunīs who were bathing in the river Aciravatī together with lay women. They pointed with their fingers at each other, and when they saw the bhikkhunīs’ breasts, hips, bellies, legs, shanks, etc., they commented on them. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked if it was true and criticized them, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，不畜洗裙者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī doesn’t keep a bathing skirt, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂此法中尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means a bhikkhunī in this dhamma.

<lzh>不畜洗裙者，謂無浴裙。</lzh>
“Not keeping a bathing skirt” means not having a bathing skirt.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
