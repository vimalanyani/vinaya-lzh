<lzh>無苾芻處作長淨學處第一百二十七</lzh>
The 127th training precept: Carrying out the uposatha in a place without bhikkhus.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼與眾多尼伴遊行人間至一村中，村外有尼住處。其日是十五日長淨日，餘尼語吐羅難陀曰：「聖者！如世尊說：『苾芻尼不應無苾芻處而為長淨。』可來共往大僧寺中。」吐羅尼曰：「我閑三藏豈可不知長淨法耶？何勞更往欲為請問！」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī was traveling among the people with many bhikkhunī companions, and they arrived at a village. Outside the village there was a bhikkhunī residence. That day was the 15th day, an uposatha day. The other bhikkhunīs said to Thullanandā: “Noble one, as the World-honored One has said: ‘A bhikkhunī shouldn’t do the uposatha in a place without bhikkhus’, please come and go with us to the bhikkhu monastery.” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “I’ve learned the three baskets. How could I not know the uposatha procedure? Why bother still going and wanting to inquire?”
The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，無苾芻處作長淨者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī carries out the uposatha in a place without bhikkhus, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>無苾芻處者，謂無大僧。</lzh>
“A place without bhikkhus” means without the bhikkhu sangha.

<lzh>作長淨者，謂說波羅底木叉戒經。</lzh>
“Carrying out the uposatha” means reciting the pātimokkha precepts sutta.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
