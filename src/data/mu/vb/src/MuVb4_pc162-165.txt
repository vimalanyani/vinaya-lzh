<lzh>使正學女等揩身學處第一百六十二三四五</lzh>
The 162nd-165th training precepts: Having a sikkhamānā etc. massage one’s body.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼使餘式叉女令揩身體，被他觸時便起樂想。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問虛實，答實。訶責乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī had sikkhamānās massage her body. When they touched her, she felt pleasure. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked if it was true or false, and she answered that it was true. He criticized her, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，令式叉摩拏女揩身者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī has a sikkhamānā massage her body, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀等。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā etc.

<lzh>式叉摩拏者，謂於二年學六法、六隨法。</lzh>
“A sikkhamānā” means someone training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules for two years.

<lzh>令彼揩身便得墮罪，廣說如前。</lzh>
Making someone massage one’s body and incuring a pācittiya offense are as explained in detail before.

<lzh>如是若使求寂女及諸俗女外道女揩身者，准前問答結罪應知。(此上是三緣)</lzh>
In the same way, if she has sāmaṇerīs, lay women, or non-Buddhist renunciate women massage her body, the dialogue and the committing of the offense should be remembered as before. (The above are three more rules.)
