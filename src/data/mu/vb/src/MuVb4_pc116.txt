<lzh>不授六學法授近圓學處第一百一十六</lzh>
The 116th training precept: Not giving the the six training rules, but giving the full ordination.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀苾芻尼，與他年滿二十童女出家，而自念曰：「若年十八，可受六法、六隨法；彼今年滿二十，何須更與二年正學法？」便受近圓。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī gave a 20 year-old girl the going forth, and thought to herself: “If she was 18, she could receive the six rules and the six supplementary rules. She’s now already 20, so why would I still need to give her the two-year sikkhamānā training?” So she gave her the full ordination. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，知童女年滿二十，不與二歲學六法、六隨法即受近圓者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī knows that a girl is 20, doesn’t give her the two-year training in the six rules and the six supplementary rules, and just gives her the full ordination, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅尼，或復餘尼，</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullā bhikkhunī, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>餘義如上。</lzh>
The rest is as above.

<lzh>釋罪相等，事並同前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
