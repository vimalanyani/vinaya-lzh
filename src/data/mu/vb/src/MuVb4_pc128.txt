<lzh>無苾芻處作安居學處第一百二十八</lzh>
The 128th training precept: Spending the rains retreat in a place without bhikkhus.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀與尼伴遊行至一聚落。此有長者大富多財，供給尼眾為造住處，彼見尼眾遂前禮敬，告言：「聖者！可於此住而作安居。」諸人聞已欲於此住，有尼報言：「聖者！如世尊說：『諸苾芻尼不應於無苾芻處作安居。』可共同往近大僧處。」吐羅尼曰：「我閑三藏，豈可不知安居法耶？何勞復往於苾芻處！」諸尼受語。安居過竟，尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā was traveling with bhikkhunī companions, and they arrived at a village. There was a householder who was rich and wealthy, and who offered to build a residence for the bhikkhunī sangha. He saw the bhikkhunī sangha, paid respect in front of them, and said: “Noble ones, please stay here and spend the rains retreat here.” When they heard this, they wanted to stay there, but a bhikkhunī said: “Noble ones, as the World-honored One has said: ‘Bhikkhunīs shouldn’t spend the rains retreat in a place without bhikkhus.’ Let’s go together to a place close to a bhikkhu sangha.” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “I’ve learned the three baskets. How could I not know the rains retreat procedure? Why bother going to the bhikkhus’ place?” The bhikkhunīs accepted what she said. When the rains retreat had passed, the bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，無苾芻處作安居者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī spends the rains retreat in a place without bhikkhus, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>無苾芻處者，義如上說。</lzh>
“A place without bhikkhus” is as explained above.

<lzh>作安居者，謂三月夏安居。</lzh>
“Spending the rains retreat” means the three months of the rains retreat.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
