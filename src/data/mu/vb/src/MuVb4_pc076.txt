<lzh>以手拍隱處學處第七十六</lzh>
The 76th training precept: Patting the hidden place with the hand.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅尼欲心熾盛，以手拍隱處，如是作時遂成瘡腫，生大苦惱，問答同前。乃至：「聖者今有何患？」彼即具說其事。諸尼曰：「合作是事應淨法耶？」報曰：「淨與非淨我已作竟。」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣如上說，乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then in Thullā bhikkhunī a lustful mind was blazing. She patted the hidden place with her hand, and when she was doing this, a boil gradually developed and great pain arose. Question and answer as before, down to: “Noble one, what do you suffer from now?” Then she fully explained the matter. The bhikkhunīs said: “Is engaging in this thing compatible with a pure state?” She replied: “Pure or impure, I’ve already done it.” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail above, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this:

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，以手拍隱處者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī pats the hidden place with her hand, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅尼，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullā bhikkhunī, or any other bhikkhunī. 

<lzh>隱處者義如上說。</lzh>
“The hidden place” is as explained above.

<lzh>拍者，謂以手拍，</lzh>
“Patting” means patting with the hand.

<lzh>墮罪如前。</lzh>
“Pācittiya” is as before.

<lzh>若尼以手拍隱處者，皆得墮罪。</lzh>
If bhikkhunīs pat the hidden place with the hand, they all incur a pācittiya offense.
