<lzh>共苾芻耳語學處第八十八</lzh>
The 88th training precept: Whispering into a bhikkhu’s ear.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時笈多尼如吐羅尼城中乞食，遂與苾芻共為耳語。諸不信者見已譏謗：「今觀此尼，非是寂靜出家，為私竊事而作期會。」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責廣如上說，乃至世尊「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Guttā bhikkhunī, like Thullā bhikkhunī, went on almsround in the city, and whispered into the ear of a bhikkhu. When people without faith had seen them, they slandered her: “Now look at this bhikkhunī. She hasn’t gone forth for extinguishment. She’s on a date for the sake of ‘the private thing’<note>i.e. sex.</note>.” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail above, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，共苾芻耳語者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī whispers into a bhikkhu’s ear, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂笈多尼，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Guttā bhikkhunī, or any other bhikkhunī. 

<lzh>共苾芻耳語者，義如上說，</lzh>
“Whispering into a bhikkhu’s ear” is as explained above.

<lzh>皆得墮罪。</lzh>
… they all incur a pācittiya offense.
