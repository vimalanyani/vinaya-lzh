<lzh>受苾芻耳語學處第八十九</lzh>
The 89th training precept: Letting a bhikkhu whisper into one’s ear.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時笈多尼如前乞食，受苾芻耳語。諸不信者見已譏嫌，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Guttā bhikkhunī as before went on almsround and let a bhikkhu whisper into her ear. When people without faith had seen them, they got upset, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，受苾芻耳語者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī lets a bhikkhu whisper into her ear, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂笈多，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Guttā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>受苾芻耳語者，義如上說，</lzh>
“Letting a bhikkhu whisper into one’s ear” is as explained above.

<lzh>皆得墮罪。</lzh>
… they all incur a pācittiya offense.
