<lzh>依時不捨羯恥那衣學處第二十三</lzh>
The 23rd training precept: Not relinquishing the kaṭhina robe at the right time.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。如世尊教令，諸苾芻尼依時應捨羯恥那衣，吐羅難陀獨不肯捨，同前有過。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛以此緣，同前集尼。問吐羅難陀：「汝實時至不欲捨羯恥那衣？」白言：「實爾。」世尊訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. The World-honored One had given this instruction: The bhikkhunīs should relinquish the kaṭhina robe at the right time. Only Thullanandā was unwilling to relinquish it. As before, she incurred a transgression. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha gathered the bhikkhunīs for this reason as before, and he asked Thullanandā: “Did you really not want to relinquish the kaṭhina robe when the right time had come?” She answered: “I really did.” The World-honored One criticized her, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this:

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>若復苾芻尼，依時不捨羯恥那衣者，泥薩祇波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī doesn’t relinquish the kaṭhina robe at the right time, it’s a nissaggiya pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>時、非時等，義如上說。</lzh>
“The right time”, “the wrong time”, etc. are as explained above.
