<lzh>使苾芻尼揩身學處第一百六十一</lzh>
The 161st training precept: Having a bhikkhunī massage one’s body.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼使餘諸尼令揩身體，他觸之時自起樂想。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問吐羅難陀：「汝實如此令尼揩身自起樂想？」答言：「實爾。」世尊訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī had other bhikkhunīs massage her body. When they touched her, she felt pleasure. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked Thullanandā: “Did you really have bhikkhunīs massage your body, and felt pleasure in this way?” She answered: “I really did.” The World-honored One criticized her, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，使苾芻尼令揩身者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī has another bhikkhunī massage her body, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀等。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā etc.

<lzh>使苾芻尼者，謂此法中近圓尼。</lzh>
“Having another bhikkhunī” means a fully ordained bhikkhunī in this dhamma.

<lzh>揩身者，謂受樂想。</lzh>
“Massaging her body” means to feel pleasure.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
