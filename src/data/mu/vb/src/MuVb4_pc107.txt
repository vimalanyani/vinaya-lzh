<lzh>輒畜弟子學處第一百七</lzh>
The 107th training precept: Just taking on a student.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。爾時世尊制諸苾芻尼滿十二歲得與他出家及受近圓。若尼雖滿十二歲，愚癡不分明不善解，而與他出家并受近圓。若自不調而欲調他，自不寂靜而欲靜他，自未超度而欲度他，自不能救而欲救他，斯等悉皆無有是處。時諸尼實無德能，輒度弟子。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛言：「尼若有力堪教弟子者，從僧伽乞，如是應與畜眾羯磨，</lzh>
Location as before. At that time, the World-honored One had laid down the precept that bhikkhunīs with 12 vassa may give others the going forth and the full ordination. Even if bhikkhunīs had 12 vassa, they were foolish, didn’t distinguish clearly, and didn’t properly understand, but they gave others the going forth and the full ordination. Since they, untamed themselves, wanted to tame others, unenlightened themselves, wanted to enlighten others, not yet crossed over themselves, wanted to make others cross over, and unable to save themselves, wanted to save others, it was all impossible. Then bhikkhunīs who were truly without virtue or capabilites just ordained students. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha said: “If a bhikkhunī has the capability to instruct students, she should ask the sangha, and they should carry out the formal act to take on students, as follows: 

<h2>Saṅghakamma</h2>

<lzh>僧伽悉集要滿十二，或復過此。彼尼隨次禮已，於上座前合掌蹲踞，作如是語：「『大德尼僧伽聽！我某甲苾芻尼夏滿十二，堪教弟子，今從尼僧伽乞畜眾羯磨。願尼僧伽與我某甲苾芻尼畜眾羯磨，哀愍故。』如是三說。次一苾芻尼作白羯磨：</lzh>
The sangha should gather everyone with 12 vassa or more. That bhikkhunī should pay respect in order, and in front of the most senior, she should join her palms, kneel down, and say: ‘Venerable bhikkhunīs, may the sangha listen. I, bhikkhunī So-and-so have 12 vassa and am capable of instructing students. I now ask the bhikkhunī sangha for the formal act to take on students. May the bhikkhunī sangha give me, bhikkhunī So-and-so, the formal act to take on students, out of compassion.’ She speaks like this three times. Then one bhikkhunī carries out the motion and the announcement:

<lzh>「『大德尼僧伽聽！此苾芻尼某甲滿十二夏，欲畜門徒。此某甲今從苾芻尼僧伽乞畜門徒法。若苾芻尼僧伽時至聽者，苾芻尼僧伽應許苾芻尼僧伽今與某甲滿十二夏畜門徒法。白如是。』次作羯磨。</lzh>
‘Venerable bhikkhunīs, may the sangha listen. This bhikkhunī So-and-so has 12 vassa, and wants to take on followers. This So-and-so has now asked the bhikkhunī sangha for the procedure for taking on followers. If the right time has come for the bhikkhunī sangha to allow this, the bhikkhunī sangha should approve that the bhikkhunī sangha now carries out the procedure for taking on followers for So-and-so, who has 12 vassa. This is the motion.’ Then she carries out the announcement:

<lzh>「『大德尼僧伽聽！此苾芻尼某甲滿十二夏，欲畜門徒，此某甲今從苾芻尼僧伽乞畜門徒法。苾芻尼僧伽今與某甲滿十二夏畜門徒法。若諸具壽聽與某甲滿十二夏畜門徒法者默然，若不許者說。』『苾芻尼僧伽已與某甲滿十二夏畜門徒法竟，苾芻尼僧伽已聽許，由其默然故，我今如是持。』」</lzh>
‘Venerable bhikkhunīs, may the sangha listen. This bhikkhunī So-and-so has 12 vassa, and wants to take on followers. This So-and-so has now asked the bhikkhunī sangha for the procedure for taking on followers. The bhikkhunī sangha now carries out the procedure for taking on followers for So-and-so, who has 12 vassa. If the long-lived ones approve to carry out the procedure for taking on followers for So-and-so, who has 12 vassa, you should remain silent. If you don’t approve, you should speak.’ ‘The bhikkhunī sangha has carried out the procedure for taking on followers for So-and-so, who has 12 vassa. The bhikkhunī sangha has approved, therefore you’re silent. I now remember it like this.’”

<h2>Origin Story (continued)</h2>

<lzh>如是世尊聽許苾芻尼有力能教弟子者從僧伽乞畜眾法。時吐羅難陀尼，未蒙僧伽與畜眾法，擅自與他出家及受近圓。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
In this way, the World-honored One allowed a bhikkhunī who is capable of instructing students to ask the sangha for the procedure for taking on students. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī, who hadn’t received the procedure for taking on students from the sangha yet, gave others the going forth and the full ordination without authorization. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>若復苾芻尼僧伽未與畜眾法，輒畜弟子者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī just takes on students when the sangha hasn’t carried out the procedure for taking on students yet, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼等如上。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” is as above. 

<lzh>僧伽未與畜眾法者，謂眾未許。</lzh>
“When the sangha hasn’t carried out the procedure for taking on students yet” means the sangha hasn’t approved it yet. 

<lzh>輒畜弟子者，謂與他出家及受近圓。</lzh>
“Just taking on students” means giving others the going forth and the full ordination. 

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained before.
