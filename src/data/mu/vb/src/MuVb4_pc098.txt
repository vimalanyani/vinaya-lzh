<lzh>知尼先在白衣家後令他去學處第九十八</lzh>
The 98th training precept: Knowing that a bhikkhunī has been staying at a lay house first, and later making her leave.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時有眾多苾芻尼遊行人間，至一聚落為求宿處，遂有長者許尼停止。時吐羅尼隨後而來，亦為求宿。村人告曰：「有餘尼眾於彼家停，聖者亦宜往彼求宿。」尼即前入告諸尼曰：「可容我宿？」諸尼報言：「此處窄狹不容。」吐羅尼曰：「隨宜即得。」諸尼聞已蹲跪相容。時吐羅尼即以手足推排舊尼，諸尼告曰：「聖者！何為如是相逼？」報曰：「不能住者，任隨意去。」諸尼議曰：「此吐羅尼盛壯多力，苦見逼迫命難存濟。」諸尼即起一時而出。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then a group of bhikkhunīs was wandering among the people, came to a village, and asked for a place to stay overnight. There was a householder who allowed bhikkhunīs to stay. Then Thullā bhikkhunī who came following behind, also asked to stay overnight. The villagers said: “The other bhikkhunīs as a group are staying in that house. It’s appropriate for the noble one to go there and ask to stay.” The bhikkhunī then went ahead, entered and told the bhikkhunīs: “Can you fit me in for the night?” The bhikkhunīs replied: “This place is narrow. We can’t fit you in.” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “If you did what was proper, you could.” When the bhikkhunīs heard this, they squatted and knelt to fit her in. Then Thullā bhikkhunī pushed and shoved the old bhikkhunīs with her hands and feet. The bhikkhunīs said: “Noble one, why are you pushing us like this?” She replied: “If you’re unable to stay, you can leave at will.” The bhikkhunīs said: “This Thullā bhikkhunī is vigorous and has much strength. It’s bitter to see the oppression, and hard to live with her.” The bhikkhunīs then got up and left at the same time. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，知苾芻尼先在白衣家，後令他去者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī knows that another bhikkhunī has been staying at a lay house first, and later makes her leave, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼等如上。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” is as above. 

<lzh>先在白衣家者，謂前到俗家。</lzh>
“Staying at a lay house first” means arriving first at a worldly house. 

<lzh>令他去者，後至令出。</lzh>
“Making her leave” means the one who arrived later makes (the first one) go out. 

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
