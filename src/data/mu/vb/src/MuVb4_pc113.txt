<lzh>不攝護學處第一百一十三</lzh>
The 113th training precept: Not supporting and protecting.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀苾芻尼，諸有女人來者皆與出家并受近圓，而不攝受衛護，隨情任去，或有出門望者、有在廊下住者、或有上閣者、或有窺窓者，晝夜如是。諸尼見譏，問言：「汝誰弟子？」答言：「是吐羅尼。」諸尼告曰：「何不聖者攝受衛護諸弟子耶？」答言：「弟子眾多何能攝受？」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī gave the going forth and the full ordination to all the women who came, but didn’t support and protect them. They just went around as they pleased: Some looked around outside the gate, some hung around on the porch, some were up on the roof of the monastery building, and some peeped through windows. It went on all day in this way. When the bhikkhunīs saw them, they were upset and asked: “Whose students are you?” They answered: “Thullā bhikkhunī’s.” The bhikkhunīs told her: “Why doesn’t the noble one support and protect these students?” She answered: “I have many students. How could I support them?” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，與他出家并受近圓，不攝受衛護者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī gives someone the going forth and the full ordination, and doesn’t support and protect her, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>出家等者，義如上說。</lzh>
“The going forth etc.” is as explained above. 

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣亦同前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
