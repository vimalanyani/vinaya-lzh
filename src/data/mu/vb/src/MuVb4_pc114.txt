<lzh>不將隨身學處第一百一十四</lzh>
The 114th training precept: Not taking away.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼與有夫主婦人出家，彼出家後白吐羅尼言：「聖者！若夫主知我出家來此，必為留難，幸願聖者將我餘方。」吐羅尼曰：「汝今何須更向餘處？捨家離俗即是餘方。」後於異時夫主來見，令脫法衣與著俗服，便將歸舍。諸苾芻尼因乞食入其舍，彼見致禮，尼便問曰：「汝今何故自還俗耶？若在佛法念念之中增長善品，今居俗累更受嬰纏。」婦人答言：「我不自由身屬於他，當時我頻諮請聖者吐羅尼，請將餘方勿遭留難；不蒙存護，今至於此。」諸尼至寺告吐羅尼言：「聖者！何故不將彼女往詣餘方，致使還俗為出家留難？」吐羅尼曰：「我無餘業，一一出家將餘方耶？」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī gave the going forth to a woman who had a husband. After she had gone forth, she said to Thullā bhikkhunī: “Noble one, if my husband finds out that I’ve gone forth and comes here, he’ll certainly create an obstacle. May the noble one take me to another place.” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “Why do you need to go to another place now? You’ve given up the household life and have left worldly affairs behind. This is already another place.” At a later time, her husband came and saw her. He made her take off the monastic robes, put on lay clothes, and then took her back home. Bhikkhunīs on almsround entered her house. When she saw them, she paid respect, and the bhikkhunīs asked her: “Why did you return to lay life now? In the Buddha’s dhamma, you were continually growing in wholesome qualities, but now you’ve again been bound by the worldly fetters.” The woman answered: “I’m not independent, I belong to someone else. Back then, I repeatedly consulted the noble Thullā bhikkhunī, and requested that she take me to another place so as not to meet with this obstacle. But I didn’t receive her protection. So now it has come to this.” The bhikkhunīs returned to the monastery and told Thullā bhikkhunī: “Noble one, why did you not take that woman to another place? You’ve caused her to return to lay life, and created obstacles for her going forth.” Thullā bhikkhunī said: “Don’t I have other business, that I can take every person who has gone forth to another place?” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. <h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，與他出家，不將隨身去者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī gives someone the going forth and doesn’t take her away with her, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>與他出家，義同上說。</lzh>
“Giving someone the going forth” is as explained above. 

<lzh>不將隨身去者，謂有難事不將餘方。</lzh>
“Not taking her away with her” means not taking her to another place if there are obstacles.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
