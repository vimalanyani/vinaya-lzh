<lzh>食竟更食學處第一百三十七</lzh>
The 137th training precept: Eating again after having eaten.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅難陀尼，晨朝持鉢入城乞食，乞得食已置於房中，隨意而食便即經行，經行既訖復來噉食，鉢中食盡縱身而臥。諸尼告曰：「聖者！食竟經行，行竟更食，食飽而臥耶？」吐羅難陀聞已，惡罵訶責餘尼。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullanandā bhikkhunī took her bowl in the morning and entered the city on almsround. Having asked for and received food, she placed it in her room, ate as much as she wanted, and then did walking meditation. Having finished her walking meditation, she returned and ate food again. When the food in her bowl was finished, she relaxed her body and lay down. The bhikkhunīs said: “Noble one, after having eaten, you did walking meditation. Having walked, you ate again. So now you’re full and laying down?” When Thullanandā heard this, she badly abused and criticized the other bhikkhunīs. The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，食竟更食者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī eats again after having eaten, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā, or any other bhikkhunī.

<lzh>食竟更食者，謂飽食後重食。</lzh>
“Eating again after having eaten” means having eaten until full and later eating again.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
