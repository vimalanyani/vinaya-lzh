<lzh>度婬女學處第一百六十</lzh>
The 160th training precept: Ordaining a prostitute.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。吐羅難陀尼度一婬女，共行乞食。諸耽色男子見已譏嫌：「此女先與俗人常行非法，今共出家者同為聚集。」尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問吐羅難陀：「汝實如此度婬女出家共行乞食？」答言：「實爾。」世尊訶責，廣說乃至「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Thullanandā bhikkhunī ordained a prostitute, and they went on almsround together. Men “who indulged in beauty” saw her and were upset: “This women previously often had sex with lay people. Now she associated with renunciates.” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked Thullanandā: “Did you really give the going forth to a prostitute and go with her on almsround in this way?” She answered: “I really did.” The World-honored One criticized her, as explained in detail, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，度婬女出家者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī gives the going forth to a prostitute, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅難陀等。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullanandā etc.

<lzh>婬女者，謂先不貞謹女人。</lzh>
“A prostitute” means a formerly disreputable woman.

<lzh>出家者，謂受求寂學處。</lzh>
“The going forth” means receiving the sāmaṇera precepts.

<lzh>釋罪相等，廣說如前。</lzh>
The explanations of the rule factors etc. are as explained in detail before.
