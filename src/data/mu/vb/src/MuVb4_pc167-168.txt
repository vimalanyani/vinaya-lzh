The 168th training precept: Massaging one’s body with sesame dregs and water.<note>These are actually precepts 167 and 168.</note>

<lzh>緣處同前，如是應知，以胡麻滓及使他以水揩身，二戒准前問答，結罪無異。</lzh>
Location as before. It should be remembered as follows: Massaging one’s body with sesame dregs, and having someone massage one’s body with water; for these two precepts the dialogue and the committing of the offense are the same as before.
