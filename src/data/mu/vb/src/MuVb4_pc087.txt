<lzh>受男子耳語學處第八十七</lzh>
The 87th training precept: Letting a man whisper into one’s ear.

<h2>Origin Story</h2>

<lzh>緣處同前。時吐羅尼如前威儀入城乞食，受男子耳語，不信敬者見已譏嫌，廣如上說，乃至與他作期會事。尼白苾芻，苾芻白佛。佛問訶責廣如上說，乃至世尊「制其學處，應如是說：</lzh>
Location as before. Then Thullā bhikkhunī as before entered the city on almsround with correct deportment. She let men whisper into her ear. When people without faith and respect had seen them, they got upset, as explained in detail above, down to: “She must be on a date with them.” The bhikkhunīs told the bhikkhus, and the bhikkhus told the Buddha. The Buddha asked and criticized, as explained in detail above, down to: “I’ll lay down another training precept. You should recite it like this: 

<h2>Final Ruling</h2>

<lzh>「若復苾芻尼，受男子耳語者，波逸底迦。」</lzh>
‘If a bhikkhunī lets a man whisper into her ear, it’s a pācittiya.’”

<h2>Explanations</h2>

<lzh>尼謂吐羅尼，或復餘尼。</lzh>
“A bhikkhunī” means Thullā bhikkhunī, or any other bhikkhunī. 

<lzh>受男子耳語者，謂將耳受男子語，</lzh>
“Letting a man whisper into one’s ear” means receiving the man’s words with her ear. 

<lzh>墮罪同前。</lzh>
“Pācittiya” is as before. 

<lzh>乃至受者皆得墮罪。</lzh>
… if they let him, they all incur a pācittiya offense.
